Why I teach this

I spent years doing everything I was supposed to do.

I started where a lot of people start — following Dave Ramsey's baby steps, working toward debt freedom, doing everything I was supposed to do.

Then life got hard. Six kids, an economic crisis, and a plan that wasn't built for what actually happens. It took years to recover and find stability again.

What pulled me forward wasn't a new budget strategy. It was a shift in how I thought about money itself. In 2022, my brother-in-law handed me Becoming Your Own Banker by R. Nelson Nash. I read it. Then I read it again.

The more I learned, the more I knew I had to share it. The moment that made it undeniable came during a live event — I heard Brandon say that he loved talking to the people he works with. Something shifted. I knew this was the work I was supposed to be doing.

That's why I built this mini-course. Not to sell you something — but because I wish someone had shown me this sooner.

— Kevin Richey
